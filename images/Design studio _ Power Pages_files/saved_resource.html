​Experience the unmatched performance of our cutting-edge, automated battery test equipment proudly made in America!

With a rich history spanning nearly four decades, Maccor has been a trusted partner in the battery industry, providing high-performance, full-featured test equipment. Our comprehensive systems offer a professional solution to meet all your testing requirements.

Comprehensive solutions for battery testing

​​Our test systems helped advanced battery technologies that have allowed people to enhance and extend their lives with implantable devices, visit other planets, walk in space, bring technology to their fingertips, and help deliver a cleaner energy source for our planet.  Whether you are making batteries, providing materials to make batteries, or using batteries as part of your product, we are here to help you to create the best device for your application.

Milestones 

Battery Materials 

Our equipment provides an analytical tool to evaluate the performance, degradation, and failure mechanisms of batteries by applying a controlled current or voltage to the battery and measuring the response. Various aspects of the battery materials can be studied...

Consumer Electronics 

Cell phones, tablets, laptops, cordless speakers, wearable technology, and many other consumer electronic devices utilize smart battery technology which our systems can interface as part of our test and development for these products...

Power Tools 

Cordless drills, saws, leaf blowers, lawn mowers, and any other battery powered tools requires safe, dependable, reliable, rechargeable battery packs.  Our equipment is used by most major manufacturers to ensure the quality of the cells and packs used in these devices.

Medical Devices 

Pacemakers, heart pumps, hearing-aides, portable x-ray machines, portable respirators , and cordless surgical equipment are just some of the applications where our equipment has advanced the technology of batteries used in medical devices..
